# Data Dictionary

## Dataset: School Dropout and Flow Rates - Brazil

### Source
INEP - Indicadores Educacionais: Taxas de Transição (2021–2022)

---

## Identification Variables

- `REGIAO`: Brazilian region  
- `UF`: State  
- `COD_MUNIC`: Municipality code  
- `MUNIC`: Municipality name  
- `AREA`: Urban or rural  
- `DEPENDENCIA`: Public or private  

---

## Educational Indicators

### Promotion Rate (TPROM)
- Progression of students to the next grade

Examples:
- `TPROM_EF`: Elementary School  
- `TPROM_EFAI`: Early Years  
- `TPROM_EFAF`: Final Years  
- `TPROM_EM`: High School  

---

### Repetition Rate (TREP)
- Students who failed and repeated the grade

---

### Dropout Rate (TEV)
- Students who abandoned school

Examples:
- `TEV_EF`: Elementary School  
- `TEV_EFAF`: Final Years  
- `TEV_EM`: High School
